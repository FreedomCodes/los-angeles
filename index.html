Click to sign up for the next event: July 25, 2pm - 6pm

Setup

Before the event, install Node.js and a workshop that you'd like to work on. If you are just starting out, learnyounode is highly recommended. Doing this before the event will help you get going quickly.

Event Details

Parking

Codesmith will be our lovely hosts for this event and they are located at 5300 Beethoven St, 90066 (map). There will be ample parking under the building or in the cul-de-sac outside marked Visitor Parking. Once parked, take the elevator in the parking area up to the PH level.

Special Talk

Will Sentance, Lead Instructor at Codesmith will be giving a 30 minute talk before the NodeSchool event, starting at 1:30pm. The talk will cover client-server interaction, request and response objects, core node elements (v8 engine, http, ftp, file system), as well as modules, npm, and require. If you'd like to attend, arrive early!

After

After the event at 6pm, Codesmith will be hosting drinks! When you're finished coding, relax and have a beer with fellow members of the community!
